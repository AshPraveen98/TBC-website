Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

This Privacy Notice for The Bakers Company LLP ('we', 'us', or 'our') describes how and why we might collect, store, use, and/or share your personal information when you use our services, including when you visit tbcarch.in. Questions? Contact us at privacy@tbcarch.in.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

We collect personal information you voluntarily provide, including names, phone numbers, email addresses, and contact data. We also automatically collect certain device and usage information (IP address, browser type, location) through cookies and similar technologies.

2. How Do We Process Your Information?

We process your information to provide and improve our Services, communicate with you, manage enquiries, and for security and fraud prevention. We process your information only when we have a valid legal reason to do so.

3. When and With Whom Do We Share Your Information?

We may share your data with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, including Google Analytics (web analytics) and Google AdSense (advertising). We may also share information during a business transfer or acquisition.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to maintain security, save preferences, and assist with analytics. We use Google Analytics, including Demographics and Interests Reporting. To opt out, visit Google's opt-out page.

5. How Long Do We Keep Your Information?

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this notice, or as required by law. When no longer needed, we delete or anonymise it.

6. How Do We Keep Your Information Safe?

We implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures. However, no electronic transmission over the internet is 100% secure. You access our Services at your own risk.

7. Do We Collect Information from Minors?

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18. By using our Services, you confirm you are at least 18 years old. Contact us at contact@tbcarch.in if you believe we have collected data from a minor.

8. Your Privacy Rights

You may withdraw consent, request access to your data, correct inaccuracies, or request deletion at any time by contacting us at privacy@tbcarch.in. You may also manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

9. Do-Not-Track Features

We do not currently respond to DNT browser signals as no uniform standard has been finalised. If a standard is adopted in the future, we will update this notice accordingly.

10. Updates to This Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the revised date at the top. We encourage you to review this notice periodically.
